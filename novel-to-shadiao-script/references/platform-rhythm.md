# Platform Rhythm

Write for short-video retention while keeping production compatible with 沙雕动画小助手.

## Shared Rhythm

- First 3-5 seconds: conflict, reversal, danger, humiliation, or absurd image.
- First 15 seconds: viewer should know who is in trouble and why it matters.
- Every 10-20 seconds: add a laugh point, plot point, status change, or suspense question.
- Middle: alternate pressure and release.
- End: reveal a stronger threat, expose a secret, or stop before the payoff completes.

## Platform Notes

### 抖音/快手

- Strong opening contradiction.
- Faster narration.
- Clear face-slap or comeback.
- Ending hook should be blunt and easy to understand.

### B站

- Can tolerate slightly richer setup.
- Meta jokes and narrator personality can be stronger.
- Character continuity matters more across episodes.

### 视频号

- Prefer clearer plot and less dense slang.
- Family, workplace, historical, and moral reversal stories work well.

## Hook Patterns

- Public humiliation: "所有人都等着他出丑。"
- Impossible task: "三分钟内，他必须证明自己不是废物。"
- Secret identity: "他们不知道，眼前这个保安就是幕后老板。"
- Sudden danger: "门外的怪物已经学会敲门。"
- Absurd reversal: "他刚准备认输，系统先投降了。"

## Ending Patterns

- New stronger opponent appears.
- Protagonist's secret is half-exposed.
- A reward creates a bigger problem.
- The rescued person is not who they seem.
- The antagonist's backer arrives.
